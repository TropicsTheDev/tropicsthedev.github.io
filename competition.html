Competition

Why Compete?

While competing is not required, it is often a great way to test your skills and grow as a grappler. Each competition is a learning experience that can boost your confidence and improve your game. Even if you aren’t looking to be a professional athlete, there is always something to be gained by going out on and doing you’re best.

Where To Compete?

There are many ways find competitions in your local area. Each organization has its own set of rules and regulations. It’s important to consult the rulebook of the organization you’d like compete under to get an understanding of their point system and moves that may be illegal in that event.
